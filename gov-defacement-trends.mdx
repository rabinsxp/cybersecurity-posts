## The Patterns
For over a decade, `.gov.np` domains have been a playground for script kiddies. In waves—most notably around 2017 and 2020—groups like "Paradox Cyber Ghost" or foreign "hacktivists" have mass-defaced government homepages.

Victims range from the **Ministry of Defense** to the **Department of Passports**. Usually, the homepage is swapped with a black background, green text, and a political manifesto or a simple "Hacked by X."

## How They Do It?
Despite the dramatic headlines, these aren't "Mission Impossible" hacks. They are mostly opportunistic, automated attacks.

### 1. The Patch Gap
A surprising number of government portals run on ancient versions of Drupal, Joomla, or WordPress. Attackers don't even target the site specifically; they run scanners looking for broad CVEs (Known Vulnerabilities) across an IP range. If you're running Drupal 6 in 2023, you're not waiting to be hacked—you're inviting it.

### 2. The "Neighbor" Problem
Many ministries share the same physical web server. If one insecure site sits next to a secure one on a shared server with bad permissions (like `chmod 777` on web roots), the attacker gets into the weak one and just "hops over" to deface the others.

### 3. admin / admin123
You'd think we're past this, but weak credentials remain a massive entry point. Admin panels are often left exposed to the public internet without even a basic VPN requirement.

## Why Should We Care?
"It's just the homepage," some say. But defacement is an attack on **Reputation** and **Integrity**. If the government can't secure its digital front door, citizens lose faith in its ability to secure their tax records, ID data, and land deeds.

## The Fixes
1.  **Automate Patching**: If it can't update itself, it shouldn't be online.
2.  **Hide the Door**: The `/wp-admin` or `/admin` page should not be visible to the world. Lock it down to a government VPN IP range.
3.  **File Integrity Monitoring**: A simple script should watch `index.php`. If it changes, change it back instantly and alert the admin. Automate the defense.
